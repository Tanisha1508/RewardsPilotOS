# Trailblazer base earning

The Sample Bank Trailblazer credit card earns 2 Trailblazer Miles for every INR 100 spent on eligible purchases.

# Trailblazer travel earning

Direct spends on airlines and hotels earn 5 Trailblazer Miles per INR 100, capped at the first INR 200000 of travel spend per month. Beyond the cap, travel spend earns the base rate.

# Trailblazer earning exclusions

Fuel, rent, wallet loads, and government payments do not earn Trailblazer Miles.
