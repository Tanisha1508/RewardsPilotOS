# Axis Atlas base earning

The Axis Bank Atlas credit card earns 2 EDGE Miles per INR 100 spent on all eligible categories. A rate of 1 EDGE Mile per INR 100 applied before 2022-12-20 and is historical only. Earned miles credit within 12 days of the eligible transaction. Source: Axis Bank official Atlas Credit Card Features T&Cs PDF, retrieved 2026-07-19.

# Axis Atlas accelerated travel earning

Travel bookings earn 5 EDGE Miles per INR 100 when made on the Travel EDGE portal or directly with airlines and hotels (identified via MCC codes), capped at a cumulative INR 2,00,000 of accelerated spend per month; above the cap, travel spend earns the base 2 per INR 100. Bookings via any third-party travel agent or OTA earn only the base rate, never the accelerated rate. Source: official T&Cs PDF, retrieved 2026-07-19.

# Axis Atlas earning exclusions

Excluded from all earning: gold and jewellery, rent, wallet loads, government institution payments, insurance, fuel, utilities, and telecom. The full exclusion list is effective 2024-04-20; rent and wallet were excluded from milestone-only calculations starting 2023-03-05. Source: official T&Cs PDF.

# Axis Atlas EDGE Mile value

1 EDGE Mile is worth INR 1, redeemable via the Travel EDGE portal for flights, hotels, and experiences. Atlas uses a single redemption value, not a multi-channel structure. Source: official T&Cs PDF, retrieved 2026-07-19.

# Axis Atlas welcome bonus by issuance cohort

The welcome bonus depends on when the card was issued, and applies to paid cards only. Cards issued on or after 2024-04-20: 2,500 EDGE Miles for 1 transaction within 37 days of issuance. Cards issued between 2022-12-20 and 2024-04-20: 5,000 EDGE Miles for 1 transaction within 30 days. Cards issued before 2022-12-20: 5,000 EDGE Miles for 3 transactions within 60 days. The bonus credits within 7 days of the qualifying transaction. Source: official T&Cs PDF.

# Axis Atlas milestones

Anniversary-year milestone bonuses (spend excludes the same categories as earn exclusions; primary and add-on card spends both count): 2,500 EDGE Miles at INR 3,00,000 spend, an additional 2,500 at INR 7,50,000, and an additional 5,000 at INR 15,00,000 — a total of 10,000 milestone EDGE Miles at INR 15 lakh cumulative spend. Milestone bonuses credit within 30 days of achievement. Source: official T&Cs PDF.

# Axis Atlas tier structure

Three tiers based on anniversary-year spend: Silver (default), Gold at INR 7.5 lakh, Platinum at INR 15 lakh. Tier is evaluated annually on prior-year spend. Downgrades: Gold to Silver below INR 7.5 lakh; Platinum to Gold at INR 7.5–15 lakh, Platinum to Silver below INR 7.5 lakh. Annual renewal bonus, credited within 30 days of fee payment: Silver 0, Gold 2,500, Platinum 5,000 EDGE Miles. Source: official T&Cs PDF.

# Axis Atlas fees

The annual fee is INR 5,000 plus GST (official source). No spend-based renewal fee waiver exists — the tiered renewal bonus partially offsets the fee instead (three independent third-party sources plus a customer complaint thread confirming denial; the official T&C does not mention a waiver either way). The foreign currency markup fee is 3.5 percent plus GST, approximately 4.13 percent effective (two independent third-party sources).

# Axis Atlas transfer caps and minimum

EDGE Miles transfer to partners in two groups, capped per customer ID (not per card) per year: Group A 30,000 EDGE Miles, Group B 1,20,000 EDGE Miles, combined maximum 1,50,000 EDGE Miles. Minimum transfer is 500 EDGE Miles. Partner-specific ratios are published on the dynamically-rendered Travel EDGE portal page rather than the static T&C. Source: official T&Cs PDF, retrieved 2026-07-19.

# Axis Atlas transfer partners Group A

Standard 1:2 ratio (1 EDGE Mile = 2 partner miles/points): Singapore Airlines KrisFlyer, Air India Flying Returns, Air Canada Aeroplan, JAL Mileage Bank, Flying Blue, Club ITC, IHG One Rewards, Etihad Guest, United MileagePlus, Turkish Miles&Smiles, and Thai Royal Orchid Plus (third-party convergent sources, consistent with the official cap structure). Newer additions at an inverted 2:1 ratio (2 EDGE Miles = 1 partner mile): British Airways Avios, Finnair Plus, and Vietnam Airlines Lotusmiles (four independent sources agreeing).

# Axis Atlas transfer partners Group B

IndiGo BluChip converts at 1:2, confirmed directly on IndiGo's official site; an introductory promotional rate may apply between 2026-06-18 and 2026-08-17 per the Axis T&C, and the 1:2 figure is the standard post-promotional rate. Radisson Rewards converts at 1:2 per a detailed third-party guide with roughly 1 working day processing; an earlier community-forum source claimed 1:1, and that conflict is recorded rather than discarded. Orchid Rewards converts at 1:1, with two independent sources agreeing.

# Axis Atlas removed transfer partners

Marriott Bonvoy, Accor Live Limitless, and Qatar Airways Privilege Club were REMOVED as Atlas transfer partners on 2026-04-02 (four independent sources agreeing on the exact date). EDGE Miles can no longer be transferred to these programs. An indirect Avios workaround to Qatar via British Airways exists but is not a direct transfer option.
