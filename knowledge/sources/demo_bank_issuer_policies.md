# Voyager Points expiry policy

Voyager Points expire 36 months after the month in which they were earned. Points expiring within 60 days are listed in the monthly statement.

# Demo Bank devaluation notice policy

Demo Bank provides 90 days advance notice before changing earn rates, transfer ratios, or redemption values on the Voyager program.
