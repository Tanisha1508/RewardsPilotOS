> SYNTHETIC FIXTURE (sprint milestone): Demo Bank, Skyhigh Airways, and Grandstay Hotels are fictional. Replaced by verified issuer content in D3.

# Voyager Points transfer to Skyhigh Airways

Voyager Points transfer to Skyhigh Airways Miles at a 1:1 ratio. The minimum transfer is 2000 Voyager Points, in multiples of 1000. Transfers complete within 48 hours and cannot be reversed.

# Voyager Points transfer to Grandstay Hotels

Voyager Points transfer to Grandstay Rewards at a 2:1 ratio, meaning 2 Voyager Points convert to 1 Grandstay point. The minimum transfer is 5000 Voyager Points.
