# Trailblazer Miles transfer to Skyhigh Airways

Trailblazer Miles transfer to Skyhigh Airways Miles at a 2:1 ratio: 2 Trailblazer Miles convert to 1 Skyhigh Mile. The minimum transfer is 1000 Trailblazer Miles.

# Trailblazer Miles transfer to Grandstay Hotels

Trailblazer Miles transfer to Grandstay Rewards at a 1:1 ratio with a minimum transfer of 3000 Trailblazer Miles.
