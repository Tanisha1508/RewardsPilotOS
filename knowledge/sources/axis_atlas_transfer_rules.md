# Axis Atlas transfer caps and minimum

EDGE Miles transfer to partners in two groups, capped per customer ID (not per card) per year: Group A 30,000 EDGE Miles, Group B 1,20,000 EDGE Miles, combined maximum 1,50,000 EDGE Miles. Minimum transfer is 500 EDGE Miles. Partner-specific ratios are published on the dynamically-rendered Travel EDGE portal page rather than the static T&C. Source: official T&Cs PDF, retrieved 2026-07-19.

# Axis Atlas transfer partners Group A

Standard 1:2 ratio (1 EDGE Mile = 2 partner miles/points): Singapore Airlines KrisFlyer, Air India Flying Returns, Air Canada Aeroplan, JAL Mileage Bank, Flying Blue, Club ITC, IHG One Rewards, Etihad Guest, United MileagePlus, Turkish Miles&Smiles, and Thai Royal Orchid Plus (third-party convergent sources, consistent with the official cap structure). Newer additions at an inverted 2:1 ratio (2 EDGE Miles = 1 partner mile): British Airways Avios, Finnair Plus, and Vietnam Airlines Lotusmiles (four independent sources agreeing).

# Axis Atlas transfer partners Group B

IndiGo BluChip converts at 1:2, confirmed directly on IndiGo's official site; an introductory promotional rate may apply between 2026-06-18 and 2026-08-17 per the Axis T&C, and the 1:2 figure is the standard post-promotional rate. Radisson Rewards converts at 1:2 per a detailed third-party guide with roughly 1 working day processing; an earlier community-forum source claimed 1:1, and that conflict is recorded rather than discarded. Orchid Rewards converts at 1:1, with two independent sources agreeing.

# Axis Atlas removed transfer partners

Marriott Bonvoy, Accor Live Limitless, and Qatar Airways Privilege Club were REMOVED as Atlas transfer partners on 2026-04-02 (four independent sources agreeing on the exact date). EDGE Miles can no longer be transferred to these programs. An indirect Avios workaround to Qatar via British Airways exists but is not a direct transfer option.
