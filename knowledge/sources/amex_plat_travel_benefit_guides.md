# Amex Platinum Travel lounge access

8 complimentary domestic lounge visits per year, capped at 2 per quarter. Priority Pass international membership is included with the US$99 annual fee waived. Source: official, retrieved 2026-07-20.

# Amex Platinum Travel fuel convenience fee

At HPCL: 0 percent convenience fee for fuel transactions under INR 5,000; 1 percent at or above INR 5,000. Source: official, retrieved 2026-07-20.

# Amex Platinum Travel eligibility

Minimum annual income INR 6,00,000 (salaried or self-employed). Card issuance is restricted to 19 named cities: Delhi/NCR, Mumbai, Bangalore, Chennai, Pune, Hyderabad, Jaipur, Indore, Coimbatore, Chandigarh, Ahmedabad, Surat, Vadodara, Lucknow, Ludhiana, Nagpur, Nashik, Trivandrum, and Mysuru. Source: official, retrieved 2026-07-20.
