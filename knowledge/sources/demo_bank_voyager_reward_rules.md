# Demo Bank Voyager base earning

The Demo Bank Voyager credit card earns 2 Voyager Points for every INR 100 spent on eligible retail purchases. Earning applies per complete block of INR 100.

# Voyager accelerated portal earning

Purchases routed through the Demo Bank travel portal earn accelerated rewards: flights booked on the portal earn 5x the base rate, which is 10 Voyager Points per INR 100. Accelerated portal earning is capped at 4000 bonus points per calendar month across the portal.

# Voyager earning exclusions

Fuel purchases do not earn Voyager Points. Rent payments and wallet loads are excluded from earning on the Voyager card.
