# Voyager lounge access

The Voyager card includes unlimited domestic airport lounge access and 6 international lounge visits per year through the partner lounge network.

# Voyager milestone voucher

On reaching INR 800000 annual spend, Voyager cardholders receive a travel voucher worth INR 10000 redeemable on the Demo Bank travel portal.
